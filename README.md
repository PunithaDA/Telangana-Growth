# Telangana Growth Analysis 

## Objectives
### Explore and Understand Datasets:
Analyze Stamp Registration, Transportation, and TS-Ipass datasets to understand their attributes and categories.
Examine the time period each dataset covers.
### Trend and Pattern Analysis:
Identify trends and patterns within each department.
Highlight growth opportunities and areas that require attention.
### Correlation Analysis:
Determine correlations among the three departments.
Report on the overall growth of Telangana through insights and relevant visuals such as shape maps.
## Key Questions and Insights
### Stamp Registration
District-wise Revenue Variation:
Analyzed revenue generation from document registration across districts.
Identified the top 5 districts with the highest revenue growth in document registration between FY 2019 and 2022.
Revenue Comparison:
Compared revenue from document registration to e-stamp challans across districts.
Listed the top 5 districts where e-stamp revenue significantly exceeded document registration revenue in FY 2022.
Pattern Alteration:
Investigated changes in e-stamp challan and document registration patterns since the implementation of e-stamp challans.
Provided suggestions to the government based on findings.
Revenue Segmentation:
Categorized districts into three segments based on their stamp registration revenue generation during FY 2021-2022.
Transportation
Seasonal Sales Correlation:
Explored the correlation between vehicle sales and specific months or seasons across different districts.
Identified months or seasons with consistently higher or lower sales rates and potential driving factors (considering Fuel-Type category).
Vehicle Class Distribution:
Analyzed the distribution of vehicles by class (MotorCycle, MotorCar, AutoRickshaw, Agriculture) across districts in FY 2022.
Identified districts with a predominant preference for a specific vehicle class.
Sales Growth Analysis:
Listed the top 3 and bottom 3 districts with the highest and lowest vehicle sales growth during FY 2022 compared to FY 2021.
Compared categories: Petrol, Diesel, and Electric.
TS-Ipass
Sector Investment Analysis:
Identified the top 5 sectors with the most significant investments in FY 2022.
District Investment Analysis:
Listed the top 3 districts attracting the most significant sector investments from FY 2019 to 2022.
Analyzed factors leading to substantial investments in these districts.
Correlation Among Departments:
Explored the relationship between district investments, vehicle sales, and stamps revenue within the same district between FY 2021 and 2022.
Sectoral Investment Patterns:
Identified sectors showing substantial investment across multiple districts between FY 2021 and 2022.
### Seasonal Investment Trends:
Analyzed seasonal patterns or cyclicality in investment trends for specific sectors.
Identified sectors experiencing higher investments during particular months.
## Technologies Used
### Database: SQL for data analysis and querying.
### Visualization: Shape maps and other relevant visuals to present insights.
## Outcomes
The analysis provided comprehensive insights into the growth patterns and development trends in Telangana. The findings highlighted key growth areas, potential opportunities, and sectors needing attention, offering valuable information for government planning and decision-making.
